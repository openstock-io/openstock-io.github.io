Openstock Index

The Openstock Index has a massive ampact on the way decisions are made. For a publicly traded asset, a low index makes "buy" less attractive - surging a lower stock market price. This is fair and healthy competition - if company A aims at a higher Index than it's competitors, sustainable decisions will be made.
