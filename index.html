Openstock Index

The Openstock Index has Massive Impact on the way decisions are made. For a publicly traded asset, a low Index makes "buy" less attractive - bringing a lower stock market price. This is fair and healthy competition - if company A aims at a higher Index than some of it's competitors, it's better start doing something about it. Sustainable decisions will be taken.
